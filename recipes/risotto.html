Risotto

Description

This traditional Italian Risotto is very easy to make and tastes delicious.

Just follow the steps below and add any protein of your choice - or enjoy it just like that!

Ingredients

  • 500g Arborio Rice
  • 1l Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Saffron
  • Dried mushrooms
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water and add the cubes of chicken or vegetable stock. Set aside.
  2. In a large pan, add some oil. Once it's warm, add the rice and stir so it doen't get burned.
  3. Once the rice is translucent, add some of the stock.
  4. Stir constantly. Each time the water has evaporated, add some more.
  5. Add the saffron and the mushrooms.
  6. Once the rice is almost done, add some grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Ready to serve. Add more Parmesan cheese on top if you like.
